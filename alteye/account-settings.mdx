## Reset password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Go to your account settings from the profile menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change password">
    Enter your current password and set a new one.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Change email

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Go to your account settings from the profile menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update email">
    Enter the new email address and confirm the change.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Delete account

<Warning>
Deleting your account permanently removes your access and cannot be undone.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Go to your account settings from the profile menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Delete account">
    Confirm the deletion when prompted.
  </Step>
</Steps>
