## What this page covers

- Credits and coverage months
- Payment methods and default card
- Invoice history and downloads

## Credits

Credits are used before charging your default payment method.

## Payment methods

- Add a new card from Billing -> Payment Methods.
- Set a default card for future charges.
- Remove an old card you no longer use.

## Invoices

Use Invoice History to download past invoices by date.
