## When to use Kicks

- Remove a player who broke rules.
- Review who kicked a player and why.
- Search kick history for a SteamID or name.

## Kick a player

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the server">
    Use the server selector to scope actions to one server.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the player">
    Open the Kicks page or the Active Players list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Kick and confirm">
    Add a clear reason and confirm the action.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Review kick history

Use the global Kicks view for all servers, or a server view for a single server. Search by SteamID or name to find a specific entry quickly.

## Notifications

Configure kick alerts in [Notification settings](/alteye/server-settings/notifications).
